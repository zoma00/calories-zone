How to create a signed release AAB
1. Export signing environment variables (new names, preferred):

export CALORIES_ZONE_UPLOAD_STORE_FILE=/absolute/path/to/your-upload-key.jks
export CALORIES_ZONE_UPLOAD_STORE_PASSWORD=your-store-password
export CALORIES_ZONE_UPLOAD_KEY_ALIAS=your-key-alias
export CALORIES_ZONE_UPLOAD_KEY_PASSWORD=your-key-password

2. Build again:

./gradlew bundleRelease

3. Verify signature:

jarsigner -verify app/build/outputs/bundle/release/app-release.aab

Note: The build also accepts old variable names (ZOMBA_UPLOAD_*) for backward compatibility.
