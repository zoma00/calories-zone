This is normal. Google says a few days because identity verification is not only automatic, it can include manual review.

Why it takes time:
=====================
1. Fraud and account-abuse checks across many signals, not just your uploaded ID.
2. Matching your legal name, country, payments profile, and developer account details.
3. Regional compliance checks that differ by country.
4. Queue volume, since many new developer accounts are verified in batches.
5. Extra review if anything is unclear, such as blurry scans, slight name mismatch, expired ID, or language/transliteration differences.

Typical timeline:
======================
1. Often 2 to 7 business days.
2. Sometimes longer if Google requests another document.

What to do now:
=================
1. Wait without repeatedly resubmitting documents.
2. Watch email, including spam/junk, for verification updates.
3. Make sure account owner phone and email stay reachable.
4. Continue app setup work in parallel so you can publish quickly after approval.

If it goes beyond about 7 business days with no update, contact Play Console support from your account and ask for a verification status check.
