Calories Zone Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 7, 2026

Calories Zone is a local-first Android app for calorie calculation, meal logging, custom food tracking, and general wellness guidance. This Privacy Policy explains how the current Android app handles data.

Data Stored By The Android App

The current Android app stores the following data locally on your device when you use the app:

  • Profile details you enter, such as profile name, age, height, weight, sex, activity level, and goal.
  • Meal logs, nutrition estimates, custom foods, and related calorie or macro totals.
  • Local assistant chat messages used to show recent conversation history.

Data Collection And Sharing

The current Android app does not require an account, does not use cloud sync, does not send your profile, meal logs, custom foods, or chat messages to a server, and does not sell personal or sensitive user data.

The current Android app does not include ads, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Network Access

The Android app does not request the Android Internet permission in this version. Its core profile, meal logging, calorie calculation, and local assistant features run on-device.

Device Backup And Retention

Android backup is disabled for the app. Data remains on your device until you delete saved items in the app, clear the app storage from Android settings, or uninstall the app.

Health And Medical Disclaimer

Calories Zone is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. The app provides calorie estimates, nutrition tracking, and general wellness guidance only. Consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Browser Preview

The browser preview is separate from the Android app. It stores preview data in your browser local storage. The preview does not need an account and should not be used as the Play Console app runtime.

Children

Calories Zone is not directed to children. The app should be used by people who can understand general nutrition tracking information or by users with appropriate guidance from a parent, guardian, or qualified professional.

Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated when app functionality changes, such as adding accounts, cloud sync, analytics, external AI services, payments, or other integrations. The effective date will be updated when changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the developer contact information shown on the Calories Zone Google Play listing.
