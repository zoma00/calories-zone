Calories Zone MVP

1. Collect age, height, weight, sex, activity level, and goal.
2. Save the user profile locally on the device.
3. Estimate BMR with Mifflin-St Jeor.
4. Convert BMR into maintenance and target calories.
5. Generate a macro split for protein, carbs, and fat.
6. Log meals locally and track daily totals against the calorie target.
7. Show coach-style AI guidance from a replaceable engine.
8. Later: add a small on-device LLM for meal parsing, then Play Billing for premium features.
